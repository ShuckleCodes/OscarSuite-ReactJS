# How to Submit Your Predictions

## Step 1: Open the Website

Go to the prediction website on your phone, tablet, or computer. Your host will give you the web address.

---

## Step 2: Select Your Name

At the top of the page, you'll see a dropdown menu that says "Select your name".

1. Tap or click the dropdown
2. Find your name in the list
3. Select it

**Don't see your name?** Ask your host to add you.

---

## Step 3: Make Your Predictions

You'll see a list of award categories (like "Best Picture", "Best Actor", etc.)

For each category:
1. Look at the nominees shown
2. Tap or click on the one you think will win
3. Your selection will be highlighted

**You can change your mind!** Just tap a different nominee to switch your pick.

---

## Step 4: Check Your Progress

At the top of the page, there's a progress bar showing how many categories you've completed.

**Tip:** Try to make a prediction for every category to maximize your chances of winning!

---

## Step 5: Submit Your Predictions

When you're ready:
1. Scroll to the bottom of the page
2. Tap the **Submit Predictions** button
3. You'll see a confirmation message

**You can update your picks** anytime before the host locks predictions (usually when the show starts).

---

## Scoring

- You get **1 point** for each correct prediction
- The person with the most points wins!
- Watch the big screen to see the live scoreboard as winners are announced

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| Can't find my name | Ask the host to add you |
| Can't submit | Make sure you've selected at least one prediction |
| Page won't load | Check your wifi/internet connection |
| Predictions locked | The show has started - no more changes allowed |

---
